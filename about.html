About K&M

Our Story

K&M Exporting Company was founded with a clear mission: to deliver Egypt's finest food products to the world. With deep roots in agriculture and a passion for quality, we specialize in exporting key staples such as potatoes, onions, and sauces to global markets — including the UAE, Russia, West Africa, and Europe.

We believe that success in international trade begins with trust, consistency, and exceptional product standards. Our team works closely with farmers, processors, and logistics partners to ensure every shipment meets international expectations.

Whether you're a supermarket chain, distributor, or wholesale buyer, we are here to be your long-term, reliable supplier from Egypt.
