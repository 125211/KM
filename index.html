About K&M Exports

We are a dedicated food exporting company based in Egypt, specializing in delivering fresh and high-quality produce including potatoes, onions, and sauces to international markets.

With years of experience in the industry, we maintain rigorous quality standards while offering competitive pricing for bulk orders.
