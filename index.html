About K&M Exporter

Our Story

Founded in 2010, K&M Exporter has grown to become one of Egypt's most trusted agricultural export companies. With decades of combined experience in the industry, we specialize in delivering premium quality sauces, fresh potatoes, fresh onions, and fresh lemons to global markets.

Our products are sourced directly from carefully selected farms across Egypt's most fertile regions, ensuring maximum freshness and quality for our international clients.
