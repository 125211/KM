About K&M Exports

Founded in 2010, K&M Exports has grown to become one of Egypt's leading food export companies. With our headquarters in Cairo and extensive network of farms across the Nile Delta, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting the highest quality Egyptian agricultural products.

Our team of agricultural experts and food safety professionals ensure that all our products meet the strictest international standards. We pride ourselves on building long-term relationships with our clients worldwide by providing consistent quality and reliable service.
