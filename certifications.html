Our Certifications

At K&M, we maintain the highest international standards in quality, safety, and export compliance. Below are key certifications that reflect our commitment to excellence:

ISO 22000 – Food Safety Management

Ensures our food handling, packaging, and storage meet international food safety standards.

Global GAP (Good Agricultural Practices)

Guarantees responsible farming, traceability, and hygiene across all our agricultural supply chains.

Export License - Egypt

Official certification allowing us to export food products globally under Egyptian trade law.
